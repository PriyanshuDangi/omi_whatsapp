What you can do
⚙ Auto meeting recaps Automatic ›

Whenever Omi finishes capturing a conversation, you get a clean recap in your WhatsApp self-chat — title, summary, action items, and category. Nothing to do; it just shows up.

Where it lands
Your “Message Yourself” chat in WhatsApp
✉ Send a WhatsApp by voice Voice ›

Ask Omi to message any of your contacts or groups. The contact name is fuzzy-matched against your WhatsApp address book.

Try saying
Send a WhatsApp to John saying I’ll be 10 minutes late.
Text Mom that I made it home.
Message the Family group: dinner’s at 8.
▮▮ Share a meeting recap Voice ›

Forward the latest meeting summary to a teammate, a friend, or a group — without typing anything.

Try saying
Send today’s meeting notes to Sarah on WhatsApp.
Share the recap with the design team group.
Forward the summary to Mom on WhatsApp.
☰ Send notes to yourself Voice ›

Push the meeting notes to your own WhatsApp on demand — useful when you want a copy in your pocket without waiting for the auto recap.

Try saying
WhatsApp me the meeting summary.
Send me the recap on WhatsApp.
⏱ Set a WhatsApp reminder Voice ›

Schedule a future WhatsApp ping to yourself or a contact. Great for follow-ups, recurring nudges, and not forgetting promises.

Try saying
Remind me on WhatsApp to call John at 5 pm.
Ping the team in 2 hours: standup time.
Send Mom a WhatsApp tomorrow at 9 am to say good morning.
+ Save a contact by voice Voice ›

If Omi can’t find a name in your address book, save it on the spot. The new contact becomes available to all the tools above instantly.

Try saying
Save contact Priya with number +91 98765 43210.
Save Alex’s WhatsApp as +1 415 555 1234.
