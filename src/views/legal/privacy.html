Omi + WhatsApp Beta

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 April 2026
The short version. This is a personal side project, not a company. We store the minimum needed to keep your WhatsApp linked to Omi: your WhatsApp session keys, your contacts, and your Omi user ID. Nothing is sold, shared, or sent to third parties. You can disconnect any time by tapping Logout WhatsApp, and email us if you want every trace of your data removed.

1. Who we are

Omi + WhatsApp ("the App", "we", "us") is an independent, non-commercial side project that lets users connect WhatsApp to Omi to receive meeting recaps and send messages by voice. We are not a registered company. The project is operated by an individual based in Sehore, Madhya Pradesh, India.

The full source code of the App is open and publicly available at github.com/PriyanshuDangi/omi_whatsapp. Anything stated in this policy can be verified directly against the code.

You can reach us at priyanshudangipd@gmail.com for any privacy question or data request.

2. What we collect

To make the App work, we store the following on the server we operate:

  • WhatsApp session credentials — the encryption keys generated when you scan the QR code. These are equivalent to a WhatsApp Web session and let our server send and receive messages on your behalf. Stored on disk under sessions/<your-uid>/creds.json.
  • WhatsApp contact list — names and phone numbers from your WhatsApp address book, used to fuzzy-match names like "send a message to John" to the right contact. Stored under sessions/<your-uid>/contacts.json.
  • Manually saved contacts — any contact you add through the Manage Contacts panel.
  • Your Omi user ID (uid) — passed by Omi in every webhook and used to associate requests with your WhatsApp session.
  • Pending reminders — if you ask Omi to set a WhatsApp reminder, the text and scheduled time are stored until the reminder fires.
  • Server logs — request method, path, status code, response time, and a redacted view of request bodies. Used for debugging. Logs are rotated and old files are deleted periodically.

3. What we do not collect

  • No analytics, tracking pixels, advertising IDs, or third-party trackers.
  • No browser cookies on the setup page.
  • No persistent storage of the content of your Omi conversations or memories — memory webhook payloads are received, formatted into a WhatsApp message, sent, and discarded from memory. (A redacted reference may appear in server logs.)
  • We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any of your data with anyone.

4. Where your data lives

All data is stored on a single server we operate. We do not use any third-party database or analytics service. The server communicates directly with WhatsApp's servers (via the Baileys library) and with Omi's webhook endpoints. No other parties receive your data.

5. How long we keep it

  • Active WhatsApp session (the keys that let us send messages on your behalf): removed immediately when you tap Logout WhatsApp, or when WhatsApp revokes the link from your phone.
  • Backups of your contact list, group list, and the phone number tied to your WhatsApp account: a snapshot is kept on the server for operational and debugging purposes after logout. These are not used for any other purpose and are never shared. Email us to have them deleted permanently.
  • Manually saved contacts (added via the Manage Contacts panel): preserved across logouts so you don't have to re-enter them next time you link. Email us to delete them.
  • Pending reminders: stored until the reminder fires, then deleted. Logging out does not cancel scheduled reminders — email us if you want them cleared.
  • Server logs (request method, path, status, your Omi uid, and a redacted request body): retained for a short window for debugging, then deleted by log rotation.

6. Your rights

You can, at any time:

  • Disconnect WhatsApp yourself — open the setup page and tap Logout WhatsApp. This severs the WhatsApp Web link and removes your active session keys from the server immediately, so we can no longer send or receive messages on your behalf.
  • Request full deletion of everything tied to your account — including contact backups, saved contacts, pending reminders, and any logs containing your uid — by emailing priyanshudangipd@gmail.com. We will confirm within a reasonable time.
  • Request a copy of any data we hold about you at the same email.

7. Security

The server runs behind a reverse proxy with HTTPS. WhatsApp session files are stored with restricted filesystem permissions. That said, this is a hobby project run by one person — we make no guarantees about security and recommend you avoid using the App with a WhatsApp account that holds highly sensitive conversations.

8. Children

The App is not intended for users under 13. If you believe a child has used the App, please contact us and we will delete their data.
